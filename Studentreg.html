Welcome, Future Space Explorer!

We're excited that you're interested in joining our astronomy society. Please fill out this form so we can learn more about you and help you begin your cosmic journey.

Important Information:

Dates and Timings of Lectures: Tuesday and Thursday at 2:00 p.m. – 3:30 p.m.

How to Prepare Your Notebook:

We will be teaching the following topics. Please take a 400-page CR book and divide it as follows:

  1. Fundamental Astronomy - 50 pages
  2. Space Technology and Rocketry – 100 pages
  3. Astrophysics and Cosmology - 100 pages
  4. Observational Astronomy - 75 pages
  5. Advanced Concepts & Competitions Preparation - 75 pages

Procedure of the Society:

  • Members must attend one of the two training days. (If you are unable to participate, please notify us via WhatsApp.)
  • The notes provided must be completed and will be checked daily.
  • Every two months, you will be given a paper on the topics taught.
  • Disciplinary issues may result in removal from the association.
  • Top scorers in the “AstroMaster Challenge” will receive a valuable certificate at the SYNODIC Annual Astronomy Camp.
