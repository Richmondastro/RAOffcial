About

The Dark Edge

The Dark Edge astronomy competition, organized by the Astronomy and Space Science Association of Richmond College, began in 2009 as a collaborative night observational event with Mahinda College. Aimed at promoting astronomy and providing a platform for young enthusiasts, it evolved to include both observational and theoretical challenges, fostering a deeper passion for the cosmos. Successfully held in 2010, 2011, 2012, 2014, and 2016, the competition took an eight-year hiatus due to various reasons. Now, in 2024, Dark Edge returns, reigniting its mission to inspire and celebrate the next generation of stargazers and astronomy lovers.

How It Works

The Dark Edge '24

The Astronomy & Space Science Association of Richmond College proudly presents The Dark Edge '24, an inter-school astronomical quiz celebrating the mysteries of the cosmos.

Competition Timeline:
Online Selection Round: December 14, 2024, at 7:00 PM (via Zoom). The top 25 teams will advance to the next round.
Second Round: Held at Richmond College, where the best teams will compete for the championship.

Embark on this cosmic adventure and shine bright!

Rules and Regulations
Registration
  • Each school may register up to three teams, each consisting of five members.
  • All participants must be current students of the school they represent.
  • Teams must complete and submit their registration forms by December 10, 2024.
Basic Guidelines
  • The selection round of the competition will take place on December 14, 2024, at 7:00 PM via the Zoom platform.
  • Each team will receive a unique code, which must be used to display their names during the Zoom meeting.
  • Teams should ensure that all members join the Zoom meeting promptly by 7:00 PM.
  • The first round will consist of:
    • 50 Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQs)
    • 1 Structured Essay Question
    • 2 Essay Questions for each subject
  • Teams are expected to maintain the highest standards of integrity. Any form of cheating or inappropriate behavior will lead to immediate disqualification by the competition officials.
  • The final results of the selection round will be determined by an experienced panel of judges. These results will be deemed final and binding.
  • The top 25 teams from the selection round will advance to the second stage of The Dark Edge '24 competition.

Prepare to challenge your limits, ignite your curiosity, and showcase your brilliance in this astronomical journey!
