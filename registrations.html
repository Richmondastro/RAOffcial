Registration Instructions

Please follow the guidelines below to successfully complete the registration process for The Dark Edge '24 :

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  • Each school can register up to three teams, with each team consisting of five members.
  • Ensure that all participants are current students of the school they represent.
  • For each team member, provide the following details:
    • Full Name
    • Date of Birth
    • Email Address
    • WhatsApp Number
  • There are three sections to fill in the form. Make sure to fill all three sections.
  • Verify that all entered details are accurate and complete.
  • Submit the registration form and wait for a confirmation.

Important Notes:

  • Inaccurate or incomplete submissions may result in disqualification.
  • Each participant can only register in one team.
  • If you encounter any issues during registration, contact the organizing team (WhatsApp) for assistance.

Once the form is completed, you will receive a confirmation email with further details. Get ready to embark on an astronomical journey!
